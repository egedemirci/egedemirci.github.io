I am also interested in cinema, although not as much as music. While I was at Sabancı, I was the president of the cinema club for a long time. I can say that I am a much more mainstream audience in cinema. You can also see my movie archive on my Letterboxd account.

Apart from these, I have been in love with football since my childhood. I am a fan of Besiktas and Manchester United. My favorite periods from history are Sir Alex Ferguson's 07-08 Manchester United, which won the Champions League in Moscow, and the 2015-2017 Beşiktaş squad, which was the best team in the league and almost made it to the semi-finals of the Europa League. World Cup tournaments have a special place in my heart (2010 South Africa, 2014 Brazil), it always brings back a lot of memories from my childhood.
